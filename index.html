About Emerald Holdings

Emerald Holdings is an Irish owned Real Estate Investment Trust (REIT) with an international property portfolio. Operating out of Dublin, we provide luxury residential and commercial rental opportunities to our clients around the globe. At present our property portfolio offers our clients to live or pursue business opportunities in a host of major economic cities such as New York, Paris and Toronto.
